Community Engagement Programs

Community Wellbeing Initiatives

Alandalus property’s commercial malls continue to serve as platforms for positive social engagement, reflecting a strong sense of responsibility toward community well-being. These spaces are consistently utilized to host a wide range of awareness, health, and educational campaigns. Many of these initiatives are now recurring programs, delivered in collaboration with government agencies, universities, and health organizations.

Read More

Ongoing Community Campaigns Hosted At Our Malls Include:

  • Polio vaccination campaign with the Ministry of Health, contributing to national immunization efforts.
  • Diabetes awareness campaign with King Abdulaziz University, promoting healthy lifestyle practices.
  • Women’s health campaign with Life Doctors, focusing on early detection and preventive care.
  • Annual breast cancer awareness event with the Ministry of Health, aligned with the international day for early detection.
  • Skin cancer education drive with King Abdulaziz University, emphasizing prevention and early screening.
  • Drug abuse awareness campaign with youth associations, targeting young people and families.
  • Oral and dental health campaign with the Saudi Dental Association, offering educational sessions and basic checkups.
  • “Dawaa’ak” medication safety awareness campaign, promoting safe usage practices.
  • Epilepsy awareness campaign with King Saud University, reducing stigma and increasing public understanding.
  • Asthma prevention program with King Saud University, held on the international day for asthma.
  • Joint pain awareness event with local clinics, offering preventative insights and consultations.
  • Liver health campaign with King Saud University, on the international day for fatty liver awareness.
  • Hernia awareness drive with university hospitals, educating on symptoms and treatment.

Social Outreach

In addition to Alandalus Property’s sustained efforts in health awareness, the Company remains actively involved in a wide range of community enrichment initiatives. These efforts extend to educational, humanitarian, and recreational activities that improve public well-being and strengthen the social fabric across its mall properties. Alandalus Property’s strategy centers on making its commercial centers accessible platforms for social value creation

  • World Sleep Day awareness booth with Nafa Charity Association, Enan Al-Ataa Volunteer Team, and the Ministry of Health.
  • Drug and substance abuse awareness session led by the General Trainer for Drug Control in the Makkah Region.
  • Seasonal influenza vaccination campaign by King Abdulaziz Hospital.
  • Clothing recycling initiative with Aoun Charity and Ghaith Charity Project.
  • “Sanad” campaign organized by King Saud bin Abdulaziz University for Health Sciences.
