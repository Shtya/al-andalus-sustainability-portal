About This Report

Alandalus Sustainability Report outlines the Company’s 2024 progress in embedding sustainable practices across its operations, with a focus on environmental responsibility, social contribution, and economic resilience. Covering all operations in Saudi Arabia from January 1 to December 31, 2024, the report demonstrates Alandalus’s commitment to transparency, stakeholder engagement, and alignment with global and national frameworks, including the GRI Standards (2021), UN SDGs, and Saudi Vision 2030. Verified data from internal and public sources, supported by external consultancy, ensures accuracy while ongoing stakeholder engagement with employees, customers, investors, and communities increases trust and strengthens sustainability reporting.

Scope and Boundaries

Full-year 2024 coverage; all operations across Saudi Arabia; issued annually.

Reporting Standards

Prepared according to GRI 2021; aligned with UN SDGs and Saudi Vision 2030.

Data Collection & Verification

Data drawn from internal and public sources; verified by external consultancy.

Stakeholder Engagement

Active engagement with employees, customers, partners, investors, and communities to enhance transparency and reporting quality.
