Governance &
Risk Management

In 2024, Alandalus Property Company reinforced its governance framework by strengthening risk management, compliance, and ethical practices across all operations. The Company conducted a comprehensive assessment of corruption risks, maintained a zero-tolerance approach to bribery, and implemented robust internal and external audit mechanisms. Policies and training programs were applied to anti-bribery, anti-money laundering, and whistleblowing processes, ensuring confidential and protected channels for reporting concerns.

Governance also extended to human resources and inclusion, promoting equal pay, a safe work environment, and fair labor practices. Environmental governance was integrated into operations through clear policies on energy, water, and waste management, while sustainable procurement practices were advanced, with 30% of purchases aligned with sustainability criteria and 86% sourced locally. These measures collectively demonstrate a culture of accountability, transparency, and responsible management.
